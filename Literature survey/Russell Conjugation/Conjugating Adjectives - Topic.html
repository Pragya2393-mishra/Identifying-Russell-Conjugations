Says Robert A. elsewhere:
quote:
Whether a word is negative or positive depends upon the conjugation, viz.:

"I'm resolute, you're stubborn, he's pigheaded."
Or:
"Horses sweat, men perspire, women glow."
How about more of the same?


"I'm big-boned, you could lose a few pounds, he's obese."

"I'm direct, you're unkind, he's downright rude."
